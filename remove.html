    1. 3.1. Personal information is collected for specified and legitimate purposes;
    2. 3.2. Personal information is processed accurately and fairly;
    3. 3.3. Personal data is processed lawfully i.e. only in cases when:
      1. 3.3.1. the data subject has given his consent to abide by the rules of E-Commerce;
      2. 3.3.2. a contract is being concluded or performed where one of the parties is the data subject;
      3. 3.3.3. amala.lt is obliged to process personal data according to the law;

    5. 3.5. Personal data shall be kept for no longer than necessary for the purposes of data processing
    6. 3.6. Personal data shall only be processed by the authorized personnel.
    7. 3.7. All information on the processed personal data is confidential.
    8. 3.8. The State Data Protection Inspectorate of Lithuania shall be notified of the personal data processing in accordance with the established procedure.
  4. 4. E-shop services can be used by:
    1. 4.1. all active natural persons, older than 18 years of age;
    2. 4.2. minors between the ages of fifteen and eighteen years can use the services only with the consent of their parents, adoptive parents or guardians, unless they are self-employed or receive a scholarship;

    4. 4.4. authorized representatives of the individuals stated above.

Such statistical data will be collected and processed in such a way as to prevent the disclosure of the Customer’s identity or any other personal data that could be used to identify the Customer.
